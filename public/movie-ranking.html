The Best Movies According to Pamela

My top 3 movies of all-time

Parent Trap

I love Nancy Meyers movies and aesthetic. I find them really cozy. Specially this one which brings me back to my childhood. It's a very nostalgic movie for me.

Mamma Mia

I love the scenografy. It transports me to Greece. Ever since I saw this movie, I've wished to visit.

Nightmare before Christmas

I consider it to be mainly a Halloween movie, which is not only the best holiday. But, it's also a Christmas movie, my second favorite. I love the songs and the animations are just intriguing.
